NoBroker Hood

Resident & Society
Management Solution

Hood is a gate & security management software services for apartment societies

Objective

Design an app from scratch for the residents which connects them with guards and the society management to make their life easier and smarter inside the society.

Role

Part of a team of 2 Product designers. IA, Userflows, Product UI/Visual designs & prototyping.

How we started

It is a complex project since it is supposed to connect different entities like residents, guards and management. Data such as details of owners, residents, flats, guards, visitors, delivery persons, cab, house helps etc has to be created and transacted. This app has multiple users roles with different sets of permissions and a lot of feature sets. The app got complicated again by a complex tech stack.

Challenges

  • Building a product from scratch

  • Should be a competative product, couple of products with similar services already exists in the market

  • Short time to release the product MVP/MLP

  • No existing user data & analytics.

  • Keeping common Interfaces across platforms

  • Different types of user segmentations like demographics(age, gender, education, income) geographic, cultural etc.

Requirements

  • Simple, fast and intuitive interfaces, that can work on all platforms, with minimal platform specific varients.

  • Focusing only on primary informations and functionality in UI. Hide complexity as much as possible.

  • Clearly communicate to the user weather they need to do an action

  • Simple & functional for (many) users

Business Goals

  • To build an ecosystem of services related to housing and real estate needs from NoBroker.

  • Push the NoBroker services directly or indirectly through the platform

  • Gather datas of societies, properties, owners and tenants to target the services from NoBroker, and comes up with product/features based on it.

Sketches & Explorations

Based on the inputs from the product team we created the user flow diagrams and IA for the basic features needed for security management like inviting guests, pre- approving visitors etc. Then we moved on to wireframes and clickable prototypes. Later on we added more features like payments, forums, complaints etc.

Visual Designs

We came up with the design system which is scalable and consistent. We built it by keeping material design patterns and interactions as it core. We also made sure that the components can go well with any future modifications and will be consistent across platforms.

Projects.

Visitor Management

Problem

Pre-approve visitor's entry. Guests, delivery, cab etc by name or phone number. Track all the visitors entry information

Research

To make seamless entry for a guest we thought of reducing number of clicks in the flow. So, our goal was to ‘add name & phone number’ and ‘contact list’ and ‘frequent visitors’ in one single screen with minimal interactions.

Sketches & Explorations

By adding a swipe-able a tabs(material design tab structure) between ‘ Add number’ , ‘ Contact list. Residents can invite multiple guests at the same time, each contact we selected/entered will add to the guest list. Also, frequently visited guests will appear on the on below the header with a simple collapsible panel.

After scheduling guest entry, taking user to upcoming visitor list and shows a invited ticket to share via messaging platforms . From my visitors list, resident can cancel, reschedule the guest entry.

Forums & Polls

Problem

Space to discuss common topics related to society. Resident can create, read and discuss in forums

Research & Solution

Making forum flexible for posting for both residents and management committee. Both can read and respond at the same time. Polls are restricted for voting a resident from one flat as optional.By keeping bigger and bolder header, user can easily readable. On-time readable answers of polling.

Emergency/SOS Alert

Problem

Solve the situation when residents face/met with an emergency or accidents.

Solution

Resident can raise an alert to the security while family members also be notified. Aesthetically differentiated dark theme since user has get to know this is a different services if he tapped by mistake. Simple and user-friendly UI, able to notify guard in just two clicks.

When security receives a alert they will rush for the help to the location. Guard will receive information about resident, flat number and type of emergency.

Maintenance Payment

Problem

All societies face difficult in collecting maintenance from the owners. There are very less options for owners as well to pay maintenance using their credit/debit card. All the available solutions charge a very high transaction fees which make maintenance collection from a portal infeasible.

Goal

Reduce the number of offline bill payments, reduce the society management work load. Better financial clarity in settlements, seamless payment eco-system and complete payment tracking

Research & Solution

Maintenance is usually calculated on per Sq.ft basis or with a fixed amount. Maintenance for a apartment may also have different components like general maintenance, water, emergency fund replenishment, etc. Seamless apartment maintenance payments integrated with society dashboard.

Complaints & Tickets Management

Problem

Complaint management is currently a complex affair in societies. To get the issue resolved, a resident has to come down to the society office to register the complaint or call the respected person on duty whose number may not be with the resident.

Explorations & Solution

We categorised in to personal and community complaints, where ‘personal’ belongs to a particular apartment and where ‘community’ stands for issues affecting common to society, block or chunk of apartments.

Based on the personas and user-flow we come up with it is necessary to consider these features:

- Residents and admin will be able to post tickets from app and dashboard respectively - Both society and residents will be able to respond on tickets by comments - Admin will have fields to manage and filter these tickets - Admin will be able to view reports - Group and separate issues in general like electrical, plumbing etc - Categorise status of the complaints registered to get track - Issue posted on apartment will only be visible to the admin.

Solution

By adding a swipeble a tabs(material design tab structure) between Amenity and Classes. Priority for a residents is amenity booking.
