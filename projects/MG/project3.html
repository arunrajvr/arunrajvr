NoBroker

Building Customer Support
for NoBroker Services

Help & Support solves the problems between the customer and relationship manager

Objective

To build a robust customer support system to improve the overall experience by resolving their issues on the platform.

Role

Research, IA, Userflows, Product UI/Visual Designs & Prototyping and testing.

Currently, we get a lot of support tickets on our platform. Some of them are very basic and could have been solved by themselves by checking the support section. Most of other tickets are not categorised according to the department and managed ineffectively leading poor visibility on the user problem.

Research and Explorations

Discussing with product team about the must have features and solutions, observing how the existing support system works, talking to one of the senior relationship managers from customer support team to identify the key actions taken in a customer support resolution, checking customer support system in other consumer products like OLA, Swiggy, Uber etc were done as part of the research process.

Planning

I wrote down the key features to be included in the project. There were lots of features that could be included, but some of them were complicated which would take a lot of time for development, so we selected a few for Phase 1 and left the rest of them for Phase 2. I created the flows based on research, design has to be simplified according to usability testing. Simplified presentation of information was required.

Designs and Solutions

After approval of Low fidelity prototypes from the product team, I started working on the UI/Visual Designs.

Challenges

  • Data gathering: since very few pre-defined queries and articles were currently available.

  • Solutions should be flexible to add future updates with multiple usecases.

Solutions

  • Quick Action Card for previously purchased services which on clicking will show support quick actions for that particular service.

  • Search card where user can search for a support topic.

  • A browse topics section where user can browse through topics and select their query.

  • On opening a section if a user query is still not solved, we will show them option to call us, chat with us, email us or get a callback with their preferred callback time (with a date time selector for this).

Summary

While I was working on this project, The most important thing I’ve learned is to Make the first version of the product/feature simple. Overthinking and considering a large number of aspects could have resulted in a huge design time as well as development time.
