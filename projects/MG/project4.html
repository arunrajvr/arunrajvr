NoBroker

Redesigning Packers &
Movers Platform

How redesigning packers & movers resulted in higher convertions

Objective

Redesigning the existing platform where the user can generate instant quote by entering the movement details

Role

Team of 2 Designers. Research, IA, Visual Designs and Prototyping.

Why packers & movers needs a redesign?

The previous NoBroker packers and movers had very less conversions. Only 10% of the total users coming to the platform were being converted. Most of them were dropping either in between the steps or on the plan screens.

  • Very less conversion rate and high drop rate on plan screens

  • Lack of catchy and visible contextual headings about the movement

  • Trust deficit due to less information about the service provided

  • The brand values of the company were not clearly visible on the page.

Research

The previous landing page had 76% drop rates. A large proportion of customers dropped on the landing page. Heatmaps across the flows and interactions revealed where the users are exactly dropping and where they were converting. Some of the users felt, they were overcharged.

Sketches & Explorations

Based on research, basically from heatmap results, we got to know that we were losing more customers from the landing page and the plan page. We decided to re-arrange the actions, headings and CTA’s according to that. Layouts were to be redisgned for more customisable and informative showcase of the services.

Solutions

  • Restructured and more contextual landing page, giving information about the services. For web users, fixed fields on the right hand side.

  • Reduced number of interaction in a single step, some of them pre-filled, especially in popups

  • Add customer support handle for assistance

  • Easily navigate between home sizes, More information about the movement like truck info, types of furnitures, appliances and count included based on home size.

  • Indicators such as price surge, free cancellations, dedicated RM etc. to boost convertion rates

  • Instead of presenting plans, allowing user to select based on his needs and budget.

Visual Designs

  • Catchy and visible headings, Strong visual cues

  • Better understandable iconography. Colors based on services.

  • More interactive material design patterns and interactions

The Result

A month after the launch, we noted that the number of user conversions increased drastically. The user drop is decreased to 26% from 76%.

Summary

The redesign project mainly focused on UX research as the fundamental objective of redesign was improving conversion rate. Analysing problems that the users faced when interacting with the page using different analytical tools and user interviews helped in redesigning the flows better. Designs based on Inputs, reviews and opinions were unable to tackle real human problems associated with the product.
