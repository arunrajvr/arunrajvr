Impact of Redesigning Emailers

How redesigned emailers increased conversions.

Objective

Redesigning the existing plan selection & onboarding emailers and developing a standard design template for NoBroker emails.

Role

Research, Information Architecture, Visual Designs.

Why needs a redesign?

  • Existing emailers were really old and outdated.

  • Click rates and conversions were too low.

  • No proper, contextual CTAs.

  • The information architecture was poor without contextual content.

  • No trust building up when the user sees it for the first time.

  • The brand offerings were not clearly mentioned.

  • Long boring texts which users might not care to read.

Research

  • Currently 26.8% plan mailer open rate and 4% CTA click rates and are recorded, which is very less.

  • Articles and case studies from Medium and other Blogs

Challenges

  • Limited space, need to convey the primary message in the first pane itself.

  • Optimizing emails for Mobile.

  • App visibilty issues, like Outlook and Gmail.

Sketches & Explorations

Based on the research, I started working on mailer content structure. There are multiple emailers sent to a customer in different scenarios. So, I started working on merging a few mailers and shortening the content inside. Also sketching emailers and grouping it, making content structure similar for all the scenarios.

Designs & Solutions

  • Indicate what possible actions are available to them by adding sufficient importance to CTAâ€™s and headings.

  • Communicate the context of the situation.

  • Bigger, catchy and short headings with easily readable texts

  • Merged contexts and reduced the number mailers sent. eg: account verification, onboarding and generate password mailers.

  • Upselling other secondary services based on contexts.

  • Reusability of the same email content structure for new & other verticals to minimize the designer time on designing emailers

Project Summary

Some of the mailers are tested and most of them are effective in genrating lead. The click rate has been increased by 46% and plan open rate is 59% in one month of time. I would say contextual contents and lesser number of emails impacted mostly resulting in more converting users from mailers.
